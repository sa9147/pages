ALL ABOUT FLOWERS


There are over 300,000 different species of flowering plants in the world! All types of flowers are beautiful.

Bees take pollen from flowers and make honey. Bees are atracted to flowers because of their color. Did you know the rarest flower color is BLUE and the most common is GREEN?


The most commenly loved flower is a rose. The rose is one of the world's most common flowers, representing appreciation, love, affection, or enduring

spirits surrounded in colors and hues
and music singing at the joyous views
hope first blooms as rose in a sea
at first obliterating reality
then it fades, the flower wilts
until it dies, no longer gilt
